Boxtribute is a digital system that works hand-in-hand with our partners in the field to deliver humanitarian aid quickly and fairly.
OUR STORY   WHAT DRIVES US
Vulnerable people in emergency scenarios are often stripped of their dignity and self-determination. Assistance often comes too late, disconnected with people’s real needs. Aid workers struggle to keep up with the changing situation on the ground.

Having witnessed this first-hand, we think aid delivery works when it is supported by a system that:
Encourages solidarity between recipients and aid workers
Respects the needs of the recipients through choice
Reflects best practice in humanitarian logistics.
THAT'S WHY WE CREATED BOXTRIBUTE.
TOGETHER WE CAN MAKE A DIFFERENCE.
OUR STORY   HOW WE STARTED
Our story begins in autumn 2016, at the height of the European refugee crisis, in a camp in Northern Greece.

We are volunteers with a Norwegian NGO tasked with distributing donations like clothes, hygiene products, and food to refugees. It was chaos for everyone involved. The situation had to change.

We created a system where through our web-app, a free shop was opened where all beneficiaries could have the same fair choice. Volunteers knew what items were available and where. Coordinators gave out virtual currency and tracked what was bought with it.

It was a great success! The shop changed how volunteers and refugees interacted - it became a place where everyone liked to go. To help other vulnerable communities, we had the dream to support many other humanitarian organizations with this system so that they could experience the same success.

EMPOWER COORDINATORS
Life is hectic as a field coordinator - a misplaced item here, a person that can’t be found there, and hours of chaos can result. Switching coordinators is a difficult process that often results in critical knowledge loss from the team. Keeping it all in sync on Boxtribute makes their lives easier so they can focus on what really matters.

WHY IT WORKS   Q&A
Q: Is Boxtribute only for refugee camps?
A: Not at all! Although it is a solution that emerged from the intense need we saw in refugee detention centers, Boxtribute is meant to help support vulnerable people in any emergency and post-emergency crisis.
Q: Is Boxtribute a non-profit?
A: Yes! We are a registered Dutch foundation under the legal name Stichting Boxwise (KVK number: 76346536 | RSIN: 860596527) since December 2019.

Our purpose is to improve the situation of vulnerable people in crisis situations in an effective, sustainable, and replicable way. Vulnerable people should receive what they need in a dignified, efficient, and responsible manner. Dignity means that everyone is treated as an individual and has their self-determination respected where possible We believe that this is best accomplished by system-level infrastructure change within the humanitarian network. In particular, we aim to address the growing gap between what is needed and what is provided by empowering local actors to run large-scale cooperative relief efforts through technology. Read our full 2021 policy plan.

Our board members and their positions are listed in Who We Are. None of the board members receive financial remuneration for their roles. More details are available in the Governance section of our policy plan.
